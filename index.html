Mining Tips for Nigeria

  • Use stable power (inverter/solar if possible – NEPA can kill profits!)

  • ASIC for BTC/LTC, GPU rigs for Kaspa/Ergo if you have cards
  • Watch difficulty spikes & coin prices – mining isn't always profit
  • Start small, calculate taxes on earnings
